Important information about immunity certificates in Hungary

As you may know, due to the high rates of vaccination across Hungary (and especially in Budapest) and the low COVID infections rates, most facilities and services have reopened!

This includes:

  • Museums
  • Main sights
  • Spas
  • Restaurants
  • Pubs
  • Clubs
  • Concerts

It is important to keep in mind that the Hungarian government has introduced an “Immunity Card” system, with the card being a requirement for entry for all indoor services (including indoor seating for restaurants/pubs and museums).

A number of international documents from many countries are already being accepted (meaning you can be granted entry), with the list of documents expanding quickly. In case you are unsure whether your certificate of vaccination / immunity will be accepted in Hungary please get in touch and we will be happy to assist.

Regardless of whether you have a document certifying immunity, you can eat at restaurants’ or grab a drink at pubs’ outdoor terraces. Most restaurants and pubs were quick to adapt to the new situation, expanding the number of outdoor terraces worth a visit across the city ranging from busy to cozy.

So as a recap, the following can be visited without any restrictions:

  • Outdoor spaces of pubs
  • Outdoor spaces of restaurants
  • All public spaces

For the following, you require an immunity certificate:

  • Indoor spaces of pubs
  • Indoor spaces of restaurants
  • Museums
  • Spas
  • Clubs
  • Concerts
  • Indoor spaces of main sights
