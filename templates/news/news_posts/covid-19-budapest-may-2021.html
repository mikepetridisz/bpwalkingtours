The current COVID-19 situation in Budapest

The vaccine rollout is progressing well in Hungary, which has helped Hungary become one of the most vaccinated countries in the world (the rate of vaccination in Budapest is above the country average). Due to this, and the strict restrictions in the past, COVID infection rates are low and are in steady decline.

Based on the above, the government has recently allowed for almost all services and facilities to be reopened!

  • Museums
  • Main sights
  • Spas
  • Restaurants
  • Pubs
  • Clubs
  • Concerts

It is important to keep in mind that the Hungarian government has introduced an “Immunity Card” system, with the card being a requirement for entry for all indoor services (including indoor seating for restaurants/pubs and museums). For important details regarding immunity certificates please see our relevant post:

Important information about immunity certificates in Hungary

Regardless of whether you have a document certifying immunity, you can eat at restaurants’ or grab a drink at pubs’ outdoor terraces. Most restaurants and pubs were quick to adapt to the new situation, expanding the number of outdoor terraces worth a visit across the city ranging from busy to cozy.

While making sure you have a memorable time in Budapest with a wide range of activities is super important for us, our number one priority will always be the safety and health of our customers. All of our tour guides are fully vaccinated for ensuring the safety of our customers, and we will provide a free mask to each of our customers upon request.

We also ask each of our customers to get in touch with us in case they experience any COVID-related symptoms so we may cancel your reservation and consider possibilities for a refund.

Please stay safe and take care of your loved ones.
