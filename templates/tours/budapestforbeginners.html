Overview

Explore the most famous and breathtaking sights of Budapest in a 3-4 hours long walking tour with a local tour guide. Learn intriguing bits of the 1000 year history that shaped Hungary and forged our culture at UNESCO world heritage sites.

Take a ride with the UNESCO world heritage metro line of Budapest, to a vibrant café next to the spectacular St. Stephen's Basilica, and cross the Danube with a short boat ride after which we can walk up to Buda Castle. From there, you will be able to take stunning photos of the fabulous façade of the Parliament and walk up to the Fisherman’s Bastion.

Besides the grandiose sites, we will see so much more, such as guerilla statues, hidden gems, and amazing architecture. On the tour you will be able to ask all of your questions from our local tour guide and get help in planning activities for your stay in our lovely city, such as recommendations for eating authentic Hungarian food, buying the best souvenirs, or getting around town. All of our guides know Budapest inside out and will help you explore each and every sight in the city, even ones you may have never heard of but will still take your breath away. It is guaranteed that you will leave the city with a plethora of amazing pictures and memories to take home with you.

Our tours are not just walks around the city, our goal is to help you engage with local culture and the atmosphere while pursuing your interests. To achieve this, we personalize each of our tours as much as we can - we will even add tweaks to our route when we can to entertain your wishes.
