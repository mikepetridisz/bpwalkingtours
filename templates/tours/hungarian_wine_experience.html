Overview

You don’t have to be a wine expert to enjoy this tour, as we will be providing you with a local tour guide who specializes in wine and will take you to cool wine bars with an amazing atmosphere and full of charm. The selection is designed to tell a story about Hungarian drinking as well as winemaking traditions arching from the Roman Empire through the kings and tyrants of the Middle Ages to the Hungarians of today.
If you would like to get to know the Hungarian wine culture or you are just exploring your interests in wine, this tour is what you have been looking for.
You will taste Hungary’s three most important varietals from the three most famous wine regions and two other interesting wines from lesser-known regions with exciting aroma and flavor profiles accompanied by cheeses and freshly baked rolls. This wine tasting is the perfect opportunity for you to learn about and taste the history of Hungarian wine.

  • Starting Point:Opera House
  • Length of Tour:3-3,5 hours
  • Final Destination:Kadarka
