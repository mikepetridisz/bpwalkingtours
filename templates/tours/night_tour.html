Overview

When the sun sets and the golden lights turn on, the city is just like a diva on the stage. Budapest looks even better when she is wearing her golden dress!
Stroll through the scenic Danube side, where the illuminated promenades and the beautiful architecture will help you understand why Budapest is often called the “Pearl of the Danube”.
On the riverside, Budapest becomes calm at night, hence exploring it at this time reveals a side that many tourists do not experience. Absorb the beauty of the Parliament, the Buda Castle, the Citadel, and the famous bridges of Budapest while also turning your attention to their glittering reflections on the Danube river. It is truly the best time to go around town in peace and tranquillity to take glamorous photos and explore the romantic spots where a lot of first kisses were blown.
After our scenic walk, we will visit the area of Budapest that never sleeps: the Party District! With a quick change of atmosphere, we will visit a busy wine bar to try some amazing Hungarian wine and later also discover Szimpla Kert, arguably the coolest ruin bar in Budapest.

  • Length of Tour:3-3,5 hours
