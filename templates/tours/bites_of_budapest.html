Overview

Join us for a bite of Hungary’s gastronomy! We are rightfully proud of our world-famous Goulash, but the soup that became known as the typical dish of Hungary is just one of our delicacies. A variety of European gastronomical influences are represented in Hungarian cuisine, all infused with our national character which is best signified by Hungarian paprika, a spice of global fame. Be our guest for this Eastern European feast!
In the span of 3-hours, you will experience the flavors of Hungarian culture from the perspective of every social class, working your way up from the breakfast of the hard-working blue-collar people of the capital to the delicacies enjoyed by the elite in the late afternoon.
You will start the day off by enjoying traditional dips, spreads, and cold cuts at the Great Market Hall, working your way up to the Sunday lunch of the middle class. We will taste goulash and pálinka (the Hungarian national spirit) according to the original recipes passed down from generation to generation. Afterward comes a surprise at a traditional restaurant that is themed after a very important event of Hungarian winters. -Hint: it is not Christmas nor New Year’s Eve.
As the conclusion of our special gastronomical experience, we will enjoy desserts and dessert wine just like the Hungarian elite would.

  • Starting Point:Fővám tér
  • Length of Tour:3 hours
  • Final Destination:Szimpla
