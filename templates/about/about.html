About Us

Who we are

Our mission is to act as representatives of our beloved city, connecting our guests with the culture, history and atmosphere of Budapest

BP Walking Tours is a community of local tour guides, each passionate about their special areas, be it history, architecture, gastronomy, or wine. What brought us together is our mutual passion for Budapest and for the power of personalized experiences which can be achieved in small groups. Our aim is to help you connect to the roots of our culture through conversation and lived experience.

Each of our tour guides are accredited and hold a professional certificate.
